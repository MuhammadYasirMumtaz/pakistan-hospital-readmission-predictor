Why These Metrics Matter
AUC-ROC: 0.68

Measures how well the model separates high-risk from low-risk patients. Our 0.68 is consistent with published research benchmarks of 0.65-0.72 for hospital readmission prediction on this dataset.

Recall: 48.4%

Of all actual high-risk patients, the model correctly identifies 48.4%. In a hospital processing 1,000 patients, this catches 484 high-risk patients who need extra care before discharge.

Why Not Accuracy?

A model predicting everyone as "Low Risk" achieves 88% accuracy but catches zero high-risk patients. Accuracy is misleading on imbalanced medical datasets. AUC-ROC and Recall are the honest metrics.

Research Benchmark

Published research on this exact UCI dataset reports AUC of 0.58-0.72. Our 0.68 places us in the "Good Engineering" category, achieved through feature engineering, diagnosis grouping and CatBoost optimization.
